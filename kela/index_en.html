In Finland, the social security system aims to safeguard sufficient economic security in all life situations. The Social Insurance Institution, Kansanel√§kelaitos or Kela in Finnish, administers social security benefits on a national level.

Offical website of Kela

Social security coverage?

When you are working in Finland or move to Finland, you may get benefits from Kela and a Kela card (Kela-kortti) starting from the day of the move. Kela will determine your right to receive benefits when you file an application. Each of Kela benefits must be applied for separately, and Kela decides them on a case-bycase basis.

What do you need a Kela card for?

By presenting your Kela card at the pharmacy or at many private medical clinics, you can get a direct, on-the-spot reimbursement for your costs. Direct reimbursement means that the Kela reimbursement will be deducted directly from the cost of the medical treatment or the medicines and you pay only the remaining amount. Even though public healthcare providers will often ask you to identify yourself using a Kela card, you can access public healthcare services without a Kela card.

The Kela card is not an official ID card. There is no electronic version of the Kela card.

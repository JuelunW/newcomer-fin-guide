Post system

The most common postal companies in Finland are Posti, PostNord, Pakettipiste, and Matkahuolto.

How to return or send

  1. Packing your return items well. Reusing the same box or bag if it is in good condition.

    • If you get a shipping label

      Printing and attaching your shipping label on your parcel.

    • If you get a Helposti-koodi (6-digital code)

      Write the Helposti-koodi on your parcel.

  3. Bring your parcel to a drop-off point. It could be a parcel locker.

  4. Keep the receipt or tracking number until you get your refund.

Public Transport

Finland public transport system is managed by a combination of regional and national operators.

Grocery stores

Grocery shopping in Finland is dominated by a few major players. Some operate several different types of stores.

Driving permit

A valid driving license from any EU or EEA member state always allows you to drive in Finland, but licenses issued by other countries may have different rules.

Medical help

Emergency call: 112, for emergency situations.

Medical helpline: 116117, for urgent health matters.

Poison Information Centre: +358 800 147 111, for preventing and treating sudden poisoning.

Mobile number

A mobile phone number is essential for day-to-day life in Finland. It is highly advisable to get a local mobile number as soon as possible after arriving in Finland to ensure smooth communication and access to services.

Country code: +358
Example number: 44 989 9321
If you wan to call this number, you should dial
+358 44 989 9321 or 044 989 9321

What is Prepaid?

Prepaid means that you pay in advance for the mobile services you use. With a prepaid plan, you have more control over your spending and can avoid unexpected charges on your bill. Prepaid plans are often more flexible than postpaid plans, as you can change or cancel your plan at any time without penalty.

What is a telephone subscribtion?

A subscription plan is a type of mobile phone plan where you pay a fixed monthly fee for a set amount of data, minutes, and texts. With a subscription plan, you typically sign a contract with a mobile network provider for a set period of time, usually 12 or 24 months. Subscription plans often come with additional benefits, such as discounted or free devices, and may offer more data or minutes than prepaid plans. However, subscription plans may also come with additional fees, such as activation fees or early termination fees if you cancel your contract before the end of the agreed-upon period.

What is Mobiilivarmenne (mobile certification)?

Mobiilivarmenne is a mobile authentication service in Finland that allows users to securely access online services using their mobile phone. It is a kind of strong certifications that used widly in Finland.

Driving permit

A valid driving license from any EU or EEA member state always allows you to drive in Finland, but licenses issued by other countries may have different rules.

2 The offer, the specification

2.1 Target audience and the purpose of the site

This website is aiming to help all background newcomer to smoothly settle down in Finland. No matter if you come from Europe, Asia, America, or Australia or if you speak English, French, Spanish, Chinese, or Vietnamese, you all will get helpful and easily understand information about moving into Finland. The website provides one-stop services aiming to deliver all newcomers' needs by just one click.

2.2 Customer's starting point

Everyone who come into Finland for one year or longer stay must to fulfill a list of mandatories. This to-do list is not clearly demonstrated by official website, such as Digital and Population Data Services Agency, Kela, and police station. Each website only indicates its own to-do items. Some steps from official even make people confused. People do not know which should be done first.

When people are confused, they will search for help. However, there is no comprehensive and well-organized guide about this on internet yet until I build it.

2.3 Specifications

We always need a home page to briefly demonstrate what this website for and which topics this website cover. I divided to-do list into different topics, and each topic is illustrated separately on the home page. Users could click the topics to navigate to its detail page.

In our website, the most valuable portion is step-by-step guide. In this page, users can easily and clearly get a picture of what, when, and how they should do in each stage. The brief information is provided on this page. If they'd like to know more details, I provide a click to our detailed page about the topics.

Each topic has their own page for details. These pages point out what, when, and where you should do, and even the cost of each step. Users can be navigated to official website if they would like to.

Users can give feedback through a contact page. An about page introduces this website and I.

All pages can change language to make everyone comfortable. All pages are also responsive. The site works on a mobile phone, tablet, and computer and ever larger screen.

Finnish ID card

The Finnish police can issue identity cards to foreign citizens who reside in Finland. Driving licenses are not accepted as valid proof of identity in Finland. You can apply for an identity card at a police station or through the police's online services. Foreigner's identity cards cannot be used as travel documents officially.

What do you need an ID card for?

To prove your identity and apply for strong identification, if you are a citizen of a non-EU/EEA country. In some situations, it can be used for e-services, such as for managing your affairs in the authorities' e-services. You need to activate it before using e-services.

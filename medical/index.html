Medical help

Emergency situations

üîó Emergency Response Center official website

If you or another person are experiencing symptoms such as chest pain, difficulties with breathing, paralysis symptoms, loss of consciousness, heavy bleeding or convulsions, the situation is an emergency.

  • Call the number 112

    • You can call the number with any phone, even without a SIM card.
    • Wait patiently and do not hang up even if the line is busy.
    • Answer the questions asked and follow the instructions given.

  • OR, use the 112 Suomi mobile app

  • OR, send an emergency text message

    Send a message to the number 112 and provide the following details:

    • the municipality and address of the emergency location.
    • what has happened.
    • any additional details and your name.

Urgent situations

  • Call the medical helpline 116117

    They will tell you how quickly you should seek treatment and direct you to the right care facility. If there is no need for you to visit the health station for emergency services, you will receive care instructions by phone.


    more about the Medical Helpline

  • OR, use the Omaolo service

    When you use the Omaolo symptom checker, you will receive an assessment of your care needs and instructions. After logging in, you can send your symptom assessment to a health care professional via the service if necessary. We will contact you within two hours by phone or Omaolo message.

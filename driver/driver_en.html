Driver license

To drive in Finland, you must have a valid driver license recognized in Finland. The Finnish Transport and Communications Agency, Traficom, is an authority serving people and businesses in licence, registration and supervisory matters. A-Katsastus, Ajovarma, is Finland's leading provider of vehicle inspections, registrations and driver exams.

Offical website of Traficom

Can you drive if your license is issued in

EU or EEA Member State Yes
Contracting State Yes, in two years from you moved to Finland*
Other states recognized Yes, in one year from you moved to Finland*
Other states NOT recognized No

Your license must be valid when you are driving.
*Your driving licence need to clearly indicate the licence categories (which type of vehicles you drive) and the validity of your driving licence. Otherwise, you need an international driving licence or an official translation with your license.

    Apply for a driving licence

    1. Make sure you meet requirements

      You must have a strong identification.
      You are not under the obligation to use an alcohol interlock when driving.
      ... ...
      see full requirements

    2. Apply for a driving licence permit

      Fill in the application online in Traficom's e-Services.

    3. Pay

      Pay for the application in the service.

    4. Learning and complete the driving examination

      First, complete the Training for candidates for first driving licences (EAS). Keep the certificate you receive with you during your driving practice.

      Once you have completed all required training and passed the driving test, you will receive your driving license by mail at your address.
